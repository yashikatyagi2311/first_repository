abes engg. clg

ABES Engineering College, established in 2000, is a private college in Ghaziabad that offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, computer applications, and management. It is affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), approved by AICTE, and accredited with an 'A' Grade by NAAC. The college has experienced faculty, strong infrastructure, and a focus on holistic student development through academic and co-curricular activities.

key features

Affiliation and accreditation: Affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), approved by AICTE, and accredited by NAAC with an 'A' Grade. Programs: Offers B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA, and MBA programs. Specializations: B.Tech programs are available in specializations like CSE, AI & Machine Learning, Data Science, ECE, Electrical & Computer Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. Infrastructure: Features experienced faculty, modern classrooms, well-equipped labs, a well-stocked library, and a conducive learning environment. Holistic development: Focuses on academic excellence, human values, and personality development through various programs, including cultural clubs and incubation centers. Admissions: Admission to the B.Tech program is based on performance in JEE Main, followed by UPTAC counseling. Other admissions depend on qualifying and entrance exams, as well as in-person interviews
